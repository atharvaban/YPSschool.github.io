WELCOME! TEXTILE ENGINEERING

TEXTILE ENGINEERING

Textile engineering department of JDIET is established with an aim to nature the talent of students for serving textile field. Textile is one of the major sector of the world's economy and India's second largest industrial sector, which deals with the basic human need i.e. clothing material. Textile engineering finds its vast scope from the microstich in medical surgery to spacesuit used in fast growing space-research for exploring the universe. It also finds its advancement and application in every emerging technological fields.

This course deals with the study of manufacturing of yarns, woven & non-woven fabrics. This stream provides extensive job potential in its various fields such as spinning, weaving, chemical processing, non-woven, knitting, research, garment manufacturing, apparel merchandising, fashion industry, automotive industry, medical industry, textile machine manufacturing, textile designing, textile marketing, fiber manufacturing and Government Sectors etc.

The Textile engineering department in our Institute has well established and well equipped laboratories, highly qualified and experienced faculty members and well established library facility. The Institute takes every care of the students in providing them quality education. The department runs TESA (Textile Engg. Students Association), which gives an exposure to students for their overall development.
