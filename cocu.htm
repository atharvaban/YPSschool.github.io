  • About US

    Jawaharlal Darda Institute of Engineering & Technology was established in 1996 with the motto of providing qualitative technical education in the highly potential Vidarbha region of Maharashtra.Under the able and dynamic leadership of Shri Vijay Darda, Chairman, Lokmat Media Pvt. Ltd, & M.P. Rajya Sabha (1998-2016)..

  • About US

    The institute is marching towards fulfilment of the dreams of our beloved 'Babuji', Late Shri Jawaharlal Darda. The institute has a spacious building of 2.95 lakhs square feet spread over 17.3 acres of green plateau surrounded by tiny hillocks, trees, valleys & lake on the way to Amravati from Yavatmal.

  • About US

    DIET is an unaided engineering institute and affiliated to the Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University. Amravati and follows the course curriculum as prescribed by it.

Co-Curricular Activities

 

1) Expert Lecture
 
4. ETA & ISF of Electronics & Telecommunication Engg. Department organized an expert lecture by Ms. Siddhi Agrawal (Alumni), Software Engineer, Amdocs, Pune on the topic “Career Opourtunities in Electronics” on 14th March 2020.
 


2) Expert Lecture
 
ETA & ISF of Electronics & Telecommunication Engg. Department organized an expert lecture by Mr. Kedar K. Kolte (Alumni), Deputy Engineering Manager, Zenith Technologies, Pune on the topic “Industrial Automation in Pharmaceutical Industries” on 28th February 2020 .
 


3) Expert Lecture
 
Department of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering in association with ETA & ISF has organized an Expert Lecture on 30th August 2019 by Mr. Mayur.R. Parate, Associate Professor, IIIT, Nagpur on the topic “Computer Vision &AI”.attended by Final and Third year students
 


4) Workshop on "VLSI Design"
 
Department of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering in association with ETA club has organized a Workshop on VLSI Design using Verilog HDL by Mr. P.R.Shivakumar, Founder & CEO, Maven on 03rd July 2019 which was attended by Final year students.
 


5) Departmental Academic Meet
 
Department of Civil Engineering has arranged the Departmental Academic committee on meeting on an online platform as a part of regular academic monitoring. The committee members were Head of department Mrs. R. N. Pantawane, Teaching faculties of the committee, Architect Mr. Suhas Puri, three class representatives of second year, third year and final year along with parent representatives. The meeting was held on 17/10/2020 at 12:00 noon online on the MS Team platform.The session began with the monitoring of actions taken at the previous meeting for feedback. Various points based on online students industrial interaction, online augmented site visit, enhance immersive learning were discussed and the same is implemented for the betterment of the students.
