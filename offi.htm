  • About US

    Jawaharlal Darda Institute of Engineering & Technology was established in 1996 with the motto of providing qualitative technical education in the highly potential Vidarbha region of Maharashtra.Under the able and dynamic leadership of Shri Vijay Darda, Chairman, Lokmat Media Pvt. Ltd, & M.P. Rajya Sabha (1998-2016)..

  • About US

    The institute is marching towards fulfilment of the dreams of our beloved 'Babuji', Late Shri Jawaharlal Darda. The institute has a spacious building of 2.95 lakhs square feet spread over 17.3 acres of green plateau surrounded by tiny hillocks, trees, valleys & lake on the way to Amravati from Yavatmal.

  • About US

    DIET is an unaided engineering institute and affiliated to the Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University. Amravati and follows the course curriculum as prescribed by it.

PRINCIPAL

Dr. R. S. Tatwawadi
Ph. D. (Civil Engineering), LL.B.

Dr. R. S. Tatwawadi is having experience of 19 years in the field of teaching and learning. During this period, he also handled various administrative posts and established Civil Engineering Department at JDIET.

He is a life member of International Association of Hydrological Studies, Indian Water Works Association, Indian Water Resources Society, Hydrological Information System and Indian Society for Technical Education. He also worked as a Member in Board of Studies, SGB Amravati University (2012-2017).

His research interest included Irrigation Water Management, Water Laws, Performance Evaluation of Irrigation Projects and Project Management. He is actively involved in identifying real time problems in rural areas which can be taken up as a research project. He has attended and presented many papers in various International and National conferences and has got paper published in various reputed journals.
